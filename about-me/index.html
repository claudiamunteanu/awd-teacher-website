I am Claudia-Maria Munteanu, holding the position of Associate Professor in the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science at "Babeș-Bolyai" University. My primary focus revolves around the captivating realms of mobile programming, software engineering, and programming paradigms. For me, it transcends being just a profession; it is a genuine passion that I bring into the academic arena.

One of the aspects that brings me immense satisfaction is guiding students through the intricacies of these dynamic fields, observing their evolution from the initial conceptualization stage to the realization of noteworthy projects. Outside the conventional classroom setting, my dedication extends to staying well-informed about the most recent developments in software development, encompassing domains such as web, mobile applications, and emerging paradigms.

In my free time, I like to do all sorts of things: watch a movie or a TV show, read a good book, spending time with my friends and family or, my newest and dearest hobby, cross-stitching.
